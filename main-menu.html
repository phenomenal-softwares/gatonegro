OVERVIEW Gatonegro's life dream is to visit his favorite cities around the world. Help him gain enough XP by correctly guessing numbers required for each city within limited attempts.
The required guess number is indicated on the suitcase by the left side of the screen.
The required attempt is indicated by the right side of the screen
COINS & MICE Earn coins and mice as you proceed through the game. Mice are required to use power-ups.
Mice can be purchased at the Mouse Shop
GUESS COMMENTS The key to guess right is to follow each comment provided after a guess is entered.
Narrow down your guesses by following the prompts.
Also observe the colours of your previous guesses:
This colour means your guess is lower than the actual number
This colour means your guess is higher than the actual number
POWER-UPS The game features two power-ups: Cat Instinct and Cat Ultra Instinct.
Cat Instinct provides a range of numbers in which the number to be guessed falls.
Cat Ultra Instinct provides the precise number. This consumes relatively much mice
ACHIEVEMENTS Achievements are a great way to get coins rewards. Check them out often.
Gold flashes of your menu button notifies that an achievement reward has been unlocked
STATS Keep track of your game progress by checking your game stats on the menu
